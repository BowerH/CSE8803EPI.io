Social Determinants of Methicillin Resistant Staphylococcus aureus Bloodstream Infection Patterns in California

We integrated eight years of California MRSA surveillance with county-level SDOH data and found that MRSA risk is driven primarily by population scale, healthcare utilization, and prior-year infections, with only limited additional predictive value from most socioeconomic indicators.
